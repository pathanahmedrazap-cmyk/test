Case Study — 2024

Kinetic Architecture
Digital Experience

Redefining the interaction between static structures and digital fluidity for global architectural firms.

The Craft

  • Role Lead Product Designer
  • Timeline 6 Months Engagement
  • Technologies
    Next.js Three.js Tailwind CSS GSAP

The Problem

Traditional architectural portfolios often feel static and fail to convey the spatial dynamics of modern buildings. Our client needed a digital presence that mirrored the innovation of their physical designs—something that felt alive, responsive, and authoritative.

The core challenge was translating high-resolution CAD models and 8K renders into a performant web experience without sacrificing the intricate details that define their work.

The Solution

We developed a "living canvas" using WebGL and advanced motion libraries. The site uses scroll-triggered 3D transformations, allowing users to virtually "walk" through project blueprints.

Immersive Walkthroughs

Interactive blueprints that respond to user gaze and cursor movement.

Adaptive Content

Dynamic layouts that shift based on the project's specific geometric identity.
